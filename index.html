Why Felipix

Predictability reduces anxiety.
Felipix makes that easy.

Visual schedules, instantly

For many autistic and neurodivergent children, knowing what comes next makes all the difference. Felipix creates a clear picture schedule from plain words — no design skills, no prep time, no laminator.

Completely private

The AI runs entirely on your phone — no internet needed, no data sent anywhere. Your child's routine stays between you and the app.

Type or speak

Describe the day however feels natural — type it out or just tap the mic and talk. No rigid format, no dropdowns to fill in.

7 languages

Works in English, Swedish, Spanish, German, French, Portuguese and Arabic — useful for families where the child's home language isn't English.

How it works

Say what's happening.
Your child sees what's coming.

1

Describe the day

Type or say the plan out loud — "drive to school, then park, then home for lunch." Exactly how you'd explain it to another adult.

→
2

AI breaks it into steps

Gemma 4 runs on your phone and turns your description into a list of clear, individual activities — no internet involved.

→
3

Hand over the phone

Each activity gets its own picture card. Your child can see exactly what's happening and what comes next — without needing to read a word.

You say

"Drive to school, then walk to the park, then home for lunch and bath"
